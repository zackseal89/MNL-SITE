# MN Legal — Blog Feature Image Prompts

This document provides cinematic, authoritative, and brand-aligned image prompts for the MN Legal "Insights" section. These prompts are designed for high-end AI generation (Midjourney, DALL-E 3) or as specific briefs for professional photography.

## Brand Aesthetic Guidelines
- **Color Palette:** Deep Navy (#1a2744), Burgundy (#8b1c3f), Cream (#f5f3ef).
- **Style:** Editorial, premium, high-contrast, minimalist.
- **Tone:** Authoritative, sophisticated, "The Economist" or "Monocle" style.

---

### 1. Navigating Kenya's New Companies Act: What Directors Must Know in 2026
**Theme:** Corporate Governance & Responsibility
- **Prompt:** A low-angle, cinematic shot of a heavy mahogany boardroom table in Nairobi’s Upper Hill. A pair of executive hands in a tailored navy suit sleeve resting on a leather-bound legal folder. In the background, a blurred view of the Nairobi skyline through floor-to-ceiling glass. Lighting: Dramatic side-lighting, deep shadows, navy and burgundy accents. 8k resolution, photorealistic, editorial style.

### 2. Arbitration vs. Litigation: Choosing the Right Path for Business Disputes
**Theme:** Strategic Resolution
- **Prompt:** A conceptual, minimalist split-screen composition. On one side, a traditional wooden judge’s gavel resting on a law book in deep shadow. On the other, a modern, brightly lit architectural boardroom where two figures in silhouettes are shaking hands. Color grade: Navy blue and charcoal with a single sharp burgundy highlight. Cinematic lighting, shallow depth of field, professional legal atmosphere.

### 3. Protecting Intellectual Property in the East African Digital Economy
**Theme:** Digital Innovation & Law
- **Prompt:** An abstract, high-tech macro shot of a gold-plated circuit board merging seamlessly into the texture of an old parchment document. Subtle holographic Nairobi cityscape floating above the surface. Sharp focus on a "Registered" symbol etched in burgundy. Moody, dark navy environment with glowing cyan and gold light trails. Premium tech-law aesthetic.

### 4. Real Estate Investment in Nairobi: Navigating Sectional Properties Act
**Theme:** Property Law & Precision
- **Prompt:** An architectural, high-contrast shot of a luxury apartment building in Westlands, Nairobi. The image is overlaid with a translucent, sharp-edged blueprint showing sectional boundaries. A silver fountain pen rests on a "Certificate of Title" document in the foreground. Clean, minimalist composition, warm cream highlights against a deep navy sky. Sophisticated real estate photography.

### 5. Modernizing Employment Contracts for the Remote Workforce
**Theme:** The Evolving Workplace
- **Prompt:** A premium, "quiet luxury" home office setup in a Nairobi suburb. A high-end laptop displays a digital contract with a burgundy electronic signature line. A glass of water and a legal notepad sit on a minimalist oak desk. Soft morning light filtering through wooden slats, creating long shadows. Color palette of muted navy, cream, and natural wood. Professional, calm, and modern.

### 6. Guide to Licensing Payments in Kenya: A Strategic Approach
**Theme:** Regulatory Compliance & Strategic Finance
- **Prompt:** A cinematic, overhead "flat lay" composition. A high-end silver fountain pen rests on an official "Business License Application" document with a prominent burgundy embossed seal. Beside the document, a modern smartphone displays a sophisticated, dark-themed banking interface showing a "Transaction Confirmed" message. The scene is set on a dark, polished ebony desk with subtle navy reflections. Sharp focus, dramatic side-lighting, editorial legal/financial aesthetic. 8k, photorealistic.

### 7. How Capital Markets Licensing Affects Legal Tech Providing Investment, Crowdfunding or Securities Solutions

- **Prompt:** A sleek, high-contrast composition showing a modern tablet displaying a sophisticated "Legal Tech" crowdfunding interface with real-time growth charts and digital security tokens.
